Student Digital Planner - Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Student Digital Planner

This name reflects the core purpose of the website: to help students plan and organize their academic tasks digitally. It's simple, clear, and speaks directly to the audience.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a simple and interactive planner for students to manage their tasks, exams, and reminders. It will include a calendar, task manager, and a suggestions form. The site aims to improve student productivity and time management with a user-friendly interface.

Scenarios

  • How can I keep track of my assignments and due dates?
  • Can I save my tasks so they don't disappear when I close the browser?
